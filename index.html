Tower of Hanoi

Tower of Hanoi is a mathematical puzzle where we have three rods and n discs. The objective of the puzzle is to move the entire stack to another rod, obeying the following simple rules:

  1. Only one disc can be moved at a time.
  2. Each move consists of taking the upper disc from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack i.e. a disc can only be moved if it is the uppermost disc on a stack.
  3. No disc may be placed on top of a smaller disk.
